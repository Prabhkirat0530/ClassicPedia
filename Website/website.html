Classical Music

In the era of Electric music and DJ we often forget where we started. The music from heaven or as we say music of the soul, music which enlighten us and makes us feel another dimensions of life, music which every planet is singing, music which every life is experiencing. The classical music is the most purest form of music present today. Delivering spiritual and religious experiences the music can bring joy and happiness to anybody's life.








Classical Instuments

Accumping the music, various instruments are used to amplify the sound and emotion of the classical raag's. Instruments of strings and sticks are used to create a spiritual experience. Classical music include the sitar, sarod, surbahar, esraj, veena, tanpura, bansuri, shehnai, sarangi, violin, santoor, pakhavaj and tabla. Out of which Tabla and Harmonium are commanly used yet their are various other instruments that are rare and not widely available.
